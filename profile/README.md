## 👋 About Us

**CrayStream** is an independent indie game studio founded in 2026.

We believe that *games shine brightest when they make you feel something.*
Rather than relying on exaggerated marketing, we focus on **atmosphere**, **narrative**, and the **intricate details** that bring worlds to life.

Our goal is to craft immersive worlds that naturally draw players in and never let go.

> *Once you get hooked, you can't escape.*
